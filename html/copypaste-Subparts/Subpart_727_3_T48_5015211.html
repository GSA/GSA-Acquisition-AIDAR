Subpart 727.3—Patent Rights under Government Contracts

Parent topic: PART 727—PATENTS, DATA, AND COPYRIGHTS

727.303 Contract clause.

Contracting officers must insert the clause at 752.227-70, Patent Reporting Procedures, in all solicitations and contracts that contain the clause at (48 CFR) FAR 52.227-11, Patent Rights—Ownership by the Contractor.
