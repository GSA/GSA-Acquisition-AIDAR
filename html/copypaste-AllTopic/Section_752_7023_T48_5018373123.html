752.7023 Required visa form for USAID participants.

Link to an amendment published at 89 FR 4208, Jan. 23, 2024.

For use in any USAID direct contract which involves training of USAID participants.

Required Visa Form for USAID Participants (APR 1984)

The Contractor shall insure that any foreign student brought to the United States for training under this contract uses visa form IAP 66A “Certificate for Exchange Visitor (J-1) Status”.
